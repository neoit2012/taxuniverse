# Private Limited Company Registration Analysis (IndiaFilings Reference)

## 1. Input Requirements
### Company Details
- Proposed Names (Review 1 & Review 2)
- Main Objects of the Company (Description of business activities)
- Authorized Capital
- Paid-up Capital

### Registered Office
- Address Line 1 & 2
- City, State, PIN Code
- Email Address for Company
- Phone Number for Company

### Director/Shareholder Details (Min 2)
- Full Name
- Date of Birth
- Place of Birth
- Mobile Number & Email
- PAN Number
- Aadhaar Number
- DIN (Director Identification Number) - if available

## 2. Document Checklist
### For Each Director/Shareholder
- **PAN Card** (Mandatory)
- **ID Proof:** Voter ID / Passport / Driving License
- **Address Proof:** Bank Statement / Electricity Bill / Mobile Bill (Latest 2 months)
- **Passport Photo**

### For Registered Office
- **Utility Bill:** Electricity / Gas / Water Bill (Latest 2 months)
- **NOC:** No Objection Certificate from Owner
- **Rent Agreement:** If rented (Notarized)

## 3. User Flow
1.  **Service Selection:** User chooses "Private Limited Registration".
2.  **Payment:** User completes payment.
3.  **Name Reservation:** User submits promoter details and proposed names.
4.  **DSC Creation:** Digital Signatures are generated for directors.
5.  **Document Collection:** User uploads KYC and Office proofs.
6.  **Form Drafting:** MOA, AOA, and SPICe+ forms are prepared.
7.  **Filing:** Application submitted to MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs).
8.  **Incorporation:** COI, PAN, and TAN are issued.
9.  **Post-Incorp Compliance:** First Auditor appointment, Bank account opening advice.
