# IndiaFilings Service Catalog Analysis (Expanded)

## 1. Overview
A complete breakdown of services offered, categorized for replication.

## 2. Business Registration
### Domestic (India)
- **Startups:** Private Limited, LLP, OPC, Partnership, Sole Proprietorship.
- **NGO:** Section 8 Company, Trust Registration, Society Registration.
- **Micro-Finance:** Nidhi Company, Producer Company.
- **Public:** Public Limited Company.

### Global
- **US Incorporation:** C-Corp, LLC (Delaware/Wyoming).
- **Singapore Incorporation:** Private Limited.
- **UK Incorporation:** Ltd Company.
- **Dubai/UAE:** Freezone Company.

## 3. Registrations & Licenses
- **Government:** 
    - Startup India (DPIIT)
    - Udyam (MSME)
    - Import Export Code (IEC)
    - Digital Signature (DSC) Class 3
- **Tax:** 
    - GST Registration
    - Professional Tax (PT)
    - TAN Registration
- **Industry Specific:**
    - FSSAI (Food License) - Basic/State/Central
    - Drug License
    - Shop & Establishment (Gumasta)
    - RERA Agent Registration
    - ISO Certification
    - BIS Registration
    - Barcode Registration

## 4. Intellectual Property (IPR)
- **Trademark:**
    - Registration (Classes 1-45)
    - Objection Reply
    - Opposition
    - Renewal
    - Assignment
- **Copyright:**
    - Literary, Artistic, Sound Recording, Software.
- **Patent:**
    - Provisional & Permanent.
- **Design:**
    - Industrial Design Registration.

## 5. Taxation (Income Tax & GST)
- **Income Tax:**
    - ITR-1 to ITR-7 Filings
    - Tax Audit (if turnover > ₹1 Cr / ₹10 Cr)
    - Capital Gains Advisory
- **TDS:**
    - Quarterly Returns (24Q, 26Q, 27Q)
    - TDS Payment Assistance
    - Lower Deduction Certificate (Form 13)
- **GST:**
    - Monthly/Quarterly Filings (GSTR-1, 3B)
    - Annual Return (GSTR-9/9C)
    - LUT Filing (for Exporters)
    - E-Invoicing Support

## 6. Compliance (ROC & Annual)
- **Private Limited / OPC:**
    - Auditor Appointment (ADT-1)
    - Annual Filing (AOC-4, MGT-7)
    - KYC for Directors (DIR-3 KYC)
- **LLP:**
    - Form 11 & Form 8
- **Payroll:**
    - PF Registration & Returns
    - ESI Registration & Returns
    - Payroll Processing (Salary Slips)

## 7. Legal & HR
- **Contracts:**
    - Founders Agreement
    - Employment Agreement
    - NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)
    - Franchise Agreement
- **Notices:**
    - Legal Notice sending/replying
    - Cheque Bounce Notice
    - Winding Up Petition

## 8. Banking
- **Current Account Opening:** Partnership with major banks (ICICI, HDFC, etc.).
- **Payment Gateway:** Integration support.
