# Compliance & Annual Filing Analysis

## 1. Overview
Mandatory annual compliance for registered entities (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC) to avoid penalties and strike-off.

## 2. Private Limited Company Compliance
### Annual Filing (ROC)
- **Forms:**
    - **AOC-4:** Financial Statements (Balance Sheet, P&L). Due: 30 days from AGM.
    - **MGT-7/7A:** Annual Return (Shareholders, Directors details). Due: 60 days from AGM.
- **Prerequisites:**
    - Statutory Audit by CA.
    - Holding Annual General Meeting (AGM).
    - Director E-KYC (DIR-3 KYC).

### Event-Based Compliance
- **ADT-1:** Auditor Appointment (5 years).
- **DIR-12:** Change in Directors.
- **INC-22:** Change in Registered Office.
- **PAS-3:** Allotment of Shares.

## 3. LLP Compliance
- **Form 11:** Annual Return (Partners info). Due: 30th May.
- **Form 8:** Statement of Accounts & Solvency. Due: 30th Oct.
- **Income Tax:** ITR-5. Due: 31st July (or 31st Oct if audit required).

## 4. Payroll Compliance
- **PF Return:** Monthly (ECR filing).
- **ESI Return:** Monthly.
- **Professional Tax:** State-specific (Monthly/Annual).

## 5. User Flow for Annual Filing
1.  **Bookkeeping:** User provides bank statements/invoices.
2.  **Financial Prep:** CA prepares Balance Sheet & P&L.
3.  **Audit:** Statutory Auditor signs the reports.
4.  **Board Meeting/AGM:** Minutes drafted and signed.
5.  **Form Upload:** AOC-4 and MGT-7 filed on MCA V3 portal.
6.  **SRN Generation:** Challan paid and receipt shared.
