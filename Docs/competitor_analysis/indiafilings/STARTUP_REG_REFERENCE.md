# Startup India Registration Analysis (DPIIT)

## 1. Overview
Registration with the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) to avail benefits like tax exemptions, easier compliance, and IPR fast-tracking.

## 2. Input Requirements
### Entity Details
- **Corporate Identification Number (CIN)** / LLPIN
- **Incorporation Date** (Must be less than 10 years old)
- **Turnover Details** (Must be under â‚¹100 Cr)
- **Entity Name**

### Business Description
- **Pitch Deck / Video Link**
- **Problem Statement** (300 words)
- **Solution Proposed** (300 words)
- **Uniqueness** (300 words)
- **Revenue Model**

### Team Details
- Number of Employees
- Director/Partner Profiles (LinkedIn/Brief Bio)

## 3. Document Checklist
- **Certificate of Incorporation** (MOA/AOA not required, just the COI)
- **Brief Note on Nature of Business** (PDF)
- **List of Awards/Recognition** (Optional)
- **Patent/Trademark details** (if any)
- **Authorization Letter** (for the authorized signatory)

## 4. User Flow
1.  **Eligibility Check:** User confirms age of company and turnover.
2.  **Profile Creation:** User creates profile on Startup India portal.
3.  **Application:** User fills the recognition form.
4.  **Upload:** Pitch deck and COI are uploaded.
5.  **DPIIT Review:** Takes 2-5 days.
6.  **Certificate:** Recognition Certificate issued.
