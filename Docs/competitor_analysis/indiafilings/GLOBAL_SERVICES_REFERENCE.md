# Global Services Analysis (IndiaFilings Reference)

## 1. Overview
Services for Indian businesses looking to expand globally, primarily focusing on US and Singapore incorporation.

## 2. US Company Incorporation (C-Corp / LLC)
### Input Requirements
- **Preferred State:** Delaware (most common) or Wyoming.
- **Company Name Choices:** 3 options.
- **Director/Member Details:** Passport, Address (Indian).
- **Shareholding Pattern:** % split between members.

### Document Checklist
- **Passport:** Mandatory for all non-US residents.
- **Address Proof:** Bank Statement / Utility Bill (Indian address is fine).
- **Form SS-4:** For EIN (Employer Identification Number) application.

### Deliverables
- **Certificate of Incorporation** (from State Secretary).
- **EIN Letter** (from IRS).
- **Operating Agreement / Bylaws.**
- **Registered Agent Service** (1st year usually included).

## 3. Singapore Company Incorporation
### Input Requirements
- **Company Name:** To be checked with ACRA.
- **Paid-up Capital:** Minimum SGD 1.
- **Local Director:** Required (Nominee Director service often needed).
- **Shareholders:** Can be 100% foreign owned.

### Document Checklist
- **Passport:** For all foreign directors/shareholders.
- **Proof of Address:** Recent bank statement/utility bill.
- **KYC Form:** Detailed due diligence required by Singapore CSPs (Corporate Service Providers).

### Deliverables
- **BizProfile:** From ACRA (Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority).
- **Constitution:** Memorandum & Articles.
- **Share Certificates.**
- **First Board Resolution.**

## 4. Other Global Services
- **UK Company Incorporation:** (Ltd Company). Needs Passport + Proof of Address. Fast processing (24-48 hours).
- **Dubai / UAE Freezone Company:** Varies by Freezone (DMCC, RAK, etc.). Often requires physical visit or video KYC.
- **Trademark Registration (International):** Madrid Protocol filings.
